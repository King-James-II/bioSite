Sonya is a mother to two boys, James who is 32, and Chris who is 31. They are the same age each year for 12 days. She was called Sam when she was a kid by her grandfather although now, she only has a few people who will call her that today. She said, “My favorite people call me Momma.” She has two dogs one who loves to chase squirrels and another who is part German Sheppard. Their names are Moo Moo short for Mooch and Sassy. She is a sports fan and loves the Clemson Tigers. She’s not extremely passionate about any specific causes. Sonya is most proud of spending over 35 years working with the public. She made a great deal of money being a manager for over 20 years. She said, “I say it’s a great accomplishment for a woman with only a high school education.” She hopes to be able to retire soon and finally get to relax. Sonya gave this advice to young professionals, “Go to college and find something you’re passionate about because then you won’t have to work a day in your life.” Having worked with the public Sonya enjoyed meeting people with many different personalities and said she believes it makes her a better judge of character. She enjoyed going on vacation to the beach when she was younger although now, she loves the mountains as well. She believes the laid-back and reliable community is what she loved when she lived there. Sonya also said, “As the lyrics from Chris Young’s song voices say, say a prayer every time you lay down at night.”
